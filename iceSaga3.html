Chapter 3: Disaster

The boy has reached his destination and finally purchased an ice pop for himself. The boy started to enjoy his ice pop and rode his bicycle controlling with one hand and other to enjoy his ice pop. As he approached to bridge connecting his village a herd of angry ox blocks his way. The herd charged towards him, and the boy panicked used his two hand to control his bicycle to escape the situation. As the disaster passed, he realized he had dropped his ice pop in the river and returning home as a sad boy who escaped the disaster and learning the importance of safe riding.
