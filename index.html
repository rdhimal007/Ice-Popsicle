Ice Popsicle

Chapter 1: A Boy Forgets His favorite Summer Snack

A long time ago, in a small country called Nepal lived a boy who loved ice popsicle; a delicious summer snack easily available in the store as refrigerator were expensive and his family were poor to afford. Every Tuesday, his mother goes to shopping and gets him a ice pop; but this time her mother forgets to buy him one.
